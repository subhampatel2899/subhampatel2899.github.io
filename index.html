Biography

Mr. Subham Patel is a Ph.D. scholar at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru, in the Department of Mathematics under the IISc Mathematics Initiative. He is pursuing his research under the guidance of Prof. Ratikanta Behera (Department of Computational and Data Sciences) and co-guidance of Prof. Thirupathi Gudi (Department of Mathematics). His work focuses on wavelets in scientific computing, machine learning, and deep learning, blending mathematics and technology to tackle complex problems. He is supported by the MOE and Axis Bank Ph.D. Fellowship. He holds an Integrated M.Sc. degree in Mathematics with a Minor degree in Electrical Engineering from the National Institute of Technology (NIT) Rourkela.
